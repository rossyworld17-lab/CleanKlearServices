Services

We offer the following services.

Commercial Cleaning

Thorough cleaning for business premises including offices, shops, and public areas. Ensures a hygienic and professional environment for staff and clients.

End of Tenancy / Move-Out Cleaning

Intensive cleaning service for vacated properties, meeting landlord or agency standards. Ideal for tenants moving out or landlords preparing for new occupants.

Domestic Cleaning

Regular or one-off home cleaning tailored to your needs. Includes dusting, vacuuming, mopping, and general tidying to keep your home spotless.

Deep Cleaning

Comprehensive top-to-bottom cleaning targeting hidden dirt and grime. Ideal for spring cleaning or after long periods without professional care.

Standard Cleaning

Routine maintenance cleaning covering essential areas like kitchens, bathrooms, and living spaces. Keeps your space clean and organized consistently.

Holiday Let Cleaning

Fast and efficient turnaround cleaning between guest stays in holiday rentals. Includes linen change, sanitation, and presentation for the next arrival.

Office Cleaning

Custom cleaning for corporate and administrative spaces. Enhances workplace hygiene and supports employee well-being and productivity.

Decluttering and Laundry Services

Organize and refresh your space with clutter removal and professional laundry care. Perfect for busy households or those needing a reset.

About

Clearview Cleaning Services has the following to tell you.

  • Our Humble Beginnings

    Welcome to Clearview Cleaning Services, your trusted family-run cleaning business based in Bradford, proudly serving major cities such as Leeds, Wakefield, Huddersfield, and Halifax, along with surrounding towns and villages. At Clearview, we transformed our passion for cleanliness into a thriving business dedicated to prioritizing the health and safety of our clients. We understand that your home or office is a reflection of you, which is why we are committed to delivering exceptional value at every step.

  • Your Needs, Our Priority

    We believe that successful business relationships are built on partnership and communication. That’s why we offer a flexible service model tailored to meet your specific cleaning requirements. Whether you need a quick refresh or a thorough deep clean, you can count on us to leave your space pristine and worry-free.

  • Commitment to Integrity

    At Clearview, we take pride in our ethical business practices. Our team members undergo thorough training and background checks to ensure the highest standards of safety and professionalism. We also use carefully vetted eco-friendly products, because we care about your space and the environment.

  • Your Satisfaction is Our Mission

    We believe that the true measure of our success is defined by our customers’ satisfaction. Our aim is to consistently exceed your expectations, but don’t just take our word for it—visit our review page to see what our satisfied clients have to say! Let Clearview Cleaning Services handle your cleaning needs, allowing you to enjoy a dirt-free, worry-free environment. Discover the difference with us today!
